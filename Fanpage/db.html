Kid Goku


Kid Goku is a cheerful and fearless Saiyan raised on Earth, known for his insatiable appetite and pure-hearted nature.

He embarks on adventures seeking the Dragon Balls, showcasing immense strength, determination, and a knack for learning powerful martial arts techniques.

Kid Goku's signature weapon is the Power Pole, a magical staff that can extend to incredible lengths.

He learns the iconic Kamehameha technique from Master Roshi, becoming one of its most skilled users.

Despite his small stature, Kid Goku possesses extraordinary strength, often surprising his opponents.

His naive and curious personality leads to many humorous and heartwarming moments throughout his journey.

Son Goku(Base)


After his childhood adventures, Goku matures into a skilled fighter, training under Master Roshi and participating in the World Martial Arts Tournaments.

As an adult, he learns about his Saiyan heritage, discovering his true name, Kakarot, and his extraterrestrial origins.

Goku marries Chi-Chi and starts a family, becoming a father to Gohan and later, Goten.

His relentless training takes him beyond earthly martial arts, mastering advanced techniques like the Kaioken and Spirit Bomb under King Kai.

Goku's battles grow more intense, as he defends Earth against powerful foes like Vegeta, Frieza, and Cell.

His adventurous spirit and Saiyan instinct drive him to seek out ever-stronger opponents across the universe.

Son Goku(Super Saiyan)


As a Super Saiyan, Son Goku reaches a heightened state of power, characterized by golden hair, a glowing aura, and amplified strength.

He first achieves this transformation during his battle with Frieza on Planet Namek, driven by intense rage over Krillin's death.

Super Saiyan Goku embodies a balance of raw power and strategic combat, becoming a symbol of hope for Earth and the universe.

His relentless training and adaptability allow him to master advanced forms like Super Saiyan 2 and 3.

The transformation drains significant energy, making control and efficiency vital during prolonged battles.

Goku’s Super Saiyan form inspires others, including his son Gohan and allies like Vegeta, to unlock their own transformations.

Son Goku(Super Saiyan 2)


Son Goku achieves Super Saiyan 2 during intense training in Other World, surpassing his previous limits.

In this form, his power, speed, and combat abilities are significantly enhanced, with lightning-like energy arcs surrounding him.

Goku first showcases Super Saiyan 2 in the Majin Buu Saga, demonstrating his mastery over the transformation.

The form is characterized by sharper hair spikes, a more radiant golden aura, and a heightened intensity in battle.

Despite its immense power, Goku prefers to conserve energy and often uses it strategically rather than relying on brute force.

Super Saiyan 2 reflects Goku's relentless dedication to becoming stronger to protect his loved ones and the universe.

Son Goku(Super Saiyan 3)


Super Saiyan 3 gives Goku longer hair, no eyebrows, and a more intense appearance, marking a major power boost.

The transformation greatly enhances Goku's strength and speed, but it consumes massive amounts of energy, limiting how long he can maintain it.

Goku first uses Super Saiyan 3 in his battle against Majin Buu during the Buu Saga, showing its raw power but struggling to maintain it.

The form is also showcased in Fusion Reborn during Goku’s fight against Janemba, where it proves effective in the afterlife.

Super Saiyan 3 requires an enormous amount of energy to maintain, which is especially problematic for Goku when he's in the living world, limiting its usage during crucial battles.

As Dragon Ball Super progresses, Super Saiyan 3 becomes less relevant, overshadowed by more powerful and efficient transformations like Super Saiyan God and Blue.

Son Goku(Super Saiyan 4)


Goku’s hair turns red, his eyes become yellow, and his body becomes more muscular. He also regains his tail.

Super Saiyan 4 vastly increases Goku's strength, speed, and endurance, surpassing earlier Super Saiyan forms.

This form grants Goku better control over his energy, allowing powerful moves like the 100x Kamehameha.

The transformation connects to the Saiyan’s primal roots, requiring control over the Great Ape form.

Goku first transforms into Super Saiyan 4 during his fight with Baby in Dragon Ball GT.

The form requires a tail and control over the Great Ape state, making it less accessible than other Super Saiyan transformations.

Son Goku(Super Saiyan God)


Son Goku becomes Super Saiyan God during the Battle of Gods arc, gaining immense power by channeling the energy of five pure-hearted Saiyans.

As a Super Saiyan God, Goku’s strength, speed, and combat abilities are drastically enhanced, rivaling even powerful gods like Beerus.

His appearance changes with red hair and a slimmer, more divine aura, signaling his newfound godly power.

This form allows Goku to engage in battle with foes beyond the capabilities of regular Super Saiyan transformations.

The Super Saiyan God transformation also helps Goku tap into his inner calm and spiritual energy, elevating his fighting style.

Goku later refines this form into Super Saiyan Blue by combining Super Saiyan God power with Super Saiyan form, further increasing his combat prowess.

Son Goku(Super Saiyan God Super Saiyan)


Son Goku, in his Super Saiyan God Super Saiyan (SSGSS) form, is a powerful transformation that combines the strength of Super Saiyan God with the regular Super Saiyan form.

This form grants Goku god-like power, significantly increasing his speed, strength, and energy control.

SSGSS is first achieved through a ritual involving the energy of five other pure-hearted Saiyans, allowing Goku to tap into divine ki.

The transformation is marked by blue hair and aura, symbolizing the advanced control over ki that Goku now possesses.

Goku uses this form in intense battles against formidable opponents like Frieza, Beerus, and Zamasu.

SSGSS plays a key role in Goku's ongoing growth, as he continues to refine his abilities and push the limits of his power.

Son Goku(UI sign)


Son Goku's Ultra Instinct (UI) is a state of heightened reflexes and intuition, allowing him to move and fight without thinking.

The form is characterized by a silver aura and Goku's hair turning silver, symbolizing his advanced mastery over both mind and body.

Ultra Instinct is considered one of the most powerful techniques in the Dragon Ball universe, allowing Goku to instinctively dodge and counter attacks

Goku first achieves Ultra Instinct during the Tournament of Power while battling Jiren, pushing his limits beyond Super Saiyan Blue.

The form greatly increases Goku's combat speed and agility, making him nearly unbeatable in battle.

While extremely powerful, the Ultra Instinct form is difficult for Goku to maintain due to its high energy demands and need for perfect mental control.

Son Goku(UI)


Mastered Ultra Instinct (UI) is a transformation where Goku achieves perfect control over his body, allowing him to instinctively react without thinking.

In this state, Goku's speed and reflexes reach unimaginable levels, making him nearly unbeatable in combat.

His hair turns silver, and his aura becomes more radiant, symbolizing the immense power of Ultra Instinct.

Mastered UI grants Goku the ability to dodge even the most powerful attacks without any conscious effort, relying purely on his instincts.

This transformation pushes Goku beyond his limits, enabling him to face gods and beings of extraordinary strength.

Despite his newfound power, Goku still seeks to improve, constantly striving to master Ultra Instinct to its fullest potential.
